Your Neighborhood Mexican Restaurant

Located in the heart of Smyrna Market Village, the Corner Taqueria has been serving guests since 2012. Everyday we feature fusion gourmet tacos, homemade salsa, local craft beers and over 30 types of tequila in our welcoming nearby Mexican restaurant and tequila bar. Stop by for tacos Smyrna style while enjoying a fresh fruit purée margarita! Don’t forget to bring your kiddos, our family friendly restaurant is the perfect destination after a wish in the Smyrna Market fountain!

September 16: Taste Some of Smyrna

The annual Taste of Smyrna takes place on the beautiful Village Green in downtown Smyrna. The event is a “Festival of Delectable” featuring samples from over 30 of your favorite Smyrna area restaurants – including in years past Tin Drum Asian Kitchen, Taco Mac, Stockyard Burgers & Bones, Atkins Park, Williamson Bros. BBQ, Bruster’s Ice Cream, Taste Jamaica, Chinese Southern Belle, Bob’s Lemonade, Wade’s Fine Eatery, Chicken Salad Chick, Jo & Glo’s Hawaiian Shaved Ice, Marlow’s Tavern, Golden Krust, Carrabba’s Italian Grill, Kenny’s Great Pies, Mezza Luna Pasta & Seafood, Frozen Cow Creamery, Opa Greek Village, Maggiano’s, and more. The event also features live music adjacent to the Veteran’s Memorial. For the adults, there is a beer and wine garden. In addition, there will be live music, kids activities, and entertainment throughout the day.
